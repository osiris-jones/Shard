MEDIC: Optional Abilities
Weakness Exposure (◆) — Ranged Spell Attack
•	Range: 4 — DMG: [LV]+1 Piercing
•	On Hit: The target makes a Resistance Roll or is Vulnerable until they perform Refocus.
Shove (◆) —Melee
•	1/Round — Range: Melee
•	Target hostile character makes a Resistance Roll or becomes Prone.
Resupply (◆) — Support
•	1/Round — Range: 1
•	A target allied character performs Refocus if able.
Distant Curatives — Passive
•	The Medic’s Support Actions have Range 4.
Modern Medicine — Passive
•	The Medic’s Spell Actions aren’t Spells and don’t provoke Reactions.
Lasting Cure — Passive
•	Heal costs (◆◆) but instead provides Regeneration x where x is half the target’s missing HP.
Prophylaxis (◆◆) — Support, Spell
•	Range: 1
•	A target allied character gains Barrier [LV]+2. This Barrier doesn’t end as turns end.

BEAST: BASIC ABILITIES
Melee Attack (◆◆) — Melee Weapon Attack
•	Range: 1 — +1 Disadvantage — DMG: [LV]+4
•	On Hit: The Beast can perform Clamp as a Free Action (◇).
•	Against an Immobilized character: +1 Advantage and +3 Bonus Damage.
Clamp (◆) — Melee
•	Range: 1
•	An adjacent hostile character makes a Resistance Roll. A character that fails is Immobilized until they are no longer Adjacent to the Beast.
Thrash (◇) — Movement
•	1/Round — Perform Thrash only while a hostile character is Immobilized by Clamp.
•	The Beast and the Immobilized character simultaneously move 1 tile in any direction. This movement doesn’t provoke reactions.	

BEAST: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Rampage (◆◆◆) — Special
•	1/Encounter
•	The Beast Moves twice its SPD. During this movement: it can’t change directions, passes through hostile characters, has Damage Resistance, ignores Difficult Terrain, and hostile characters it becomes adjacent make a Resistance Roll, becoming Prone on a failure. At the end of this movement: the Beast can perform Clamp as a Free Action (◇).
Ragdoll (◆◆) — Special
•	Perform Ragdoll only if a hostile character was Immobilized by Clamp at the beginning of the Beast’s turn.
•	The Immobilized Character suffers Push 4, Stunned +1, and Chaos Damage equal to half their current HP. If it collides with another character, both characters become Prone.
Unleash (◆) — Special
•	For the rest of the encounter: The Beast has 0 Armor and can’t receive Healing or be the target of Support Actions. It has +1 SPD and gains (◆) at the beginning of each of its turns.
Imposing — Passive
•	Characters Immobilized by the Beast’s Clamp have +1 Disadvantage on Attack Rolls and Resistance Rolls.
Shatter — Passive
•	When the Beast’s Melee Attack hits a character Immobilized by Clamp: That character is Sundered for the rest of the encounter.

GUARDIAN: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Destructive Wave (◆◆) — Spell
•	Charged
•	All characters within Range 2 make a Resistance Roll, becoming Prone and Stunned +1 on a failure.
•	Adjacent allied characters have +1 Advantage on the Resistance Roll.
Bouncing Shield (◆◆) — Ranged Weapon Attack
•	Charged — Range: 3 — DMG: [LV]+3
•	On Hit: Repeat the attack with Flurry against a different character within Range 2 of the previous target.
•	( This effect can occur no more than [LV]+1 times. )
Hand of Protection (◆) — Support Spell
•	Range: 3
•	Target allied character becomes Invulnerable until the start of the Guardian’s next turn. The effect also ends if the Guardian is defeated or becomes further than Range 3.
•	While this ability grants an allied character Invulnerable: The Guardian loses Unbreakable.
Attack of Opportunity (◈) — Attack Reaction
•	1/Round — Condition: A hostile character within the Range of Heavy Attack performs a Movement Action.
•	Effect: Perform Heavy Attack on the triggering character before the triggering Action.
Mailbreaker — Passive
•	Counterattack deals Piercing Damage.
Tears of Denial — Passive
•	The first time the Guardian is hit by an Attack each round: prevent all damage and bonus damage from that Attack.


SENTINEL: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Preparation (◆◆) — Special
•	The next time the Sentinel performs Melee Attack against a target, the Sentinel performs Melee Attack twice against that target instead.
Combat Reflexes (◆◆) — Special
•	The Sentinel is Immobilized until Combat Reflexes is deactivated (◆◆).
•	While active: Attack of Opportunity is 1/Turn. ( Instead of 1/Round. )
Brace (◈) — Attack Reaction
•	1/Round — Condition: The Sentinel is dealt any amount of damage and bonus damage from a single source.
•	Effect: The Sentinel gains Damage Resistance against the triggering damage.
•	An adjacent non-Minion allied character gains +1 Advantage on their next Attack Roll.
Hamstring — Passive
•	Attack of Opportunity gains On Hit: The target is Immobilized until the start of their next turn.
Long Reach — Passive
•	Melee Attack gains +1 Range.
Mage Slayer — Passive
•	Attack of Opportunity has the following additional Condition: A hostile character within the Range of Melee Attack performs a Spell Action.
Avenger — Passive
•	Attack of Opportunity has the following additional Condition: A hostile character within the Range of Melee Attack performs an Action that targets an adjacent allied character.
Stable — Passive
•	The Sentinel ignores Difficult Terrain.

EXECUTIONER: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Throat Punch (◆) — Melee Attack
•	1/Round — Range: Melee — DMG: [LV]+2
•	Flurry
•	On Hit: The target is Silenced until the end of their next turn.
•	( A Silenced character can’t perform Spell Actions. It’s a special Condition. )
At Any Cost — Passive
•	The Executioner has Damage Resistance while adjacent to a hostile character.
Public Execution — Passive
•	When the Executioner reduces a hostile character to 0 HP, hostile characters in a Size 3 Area centered on the Executioner make a Resistance Roll. A character that fails is Immobilized until the end of their next turn.
In the Name of God — Passive
•	When the Executioner performs Headsman’s Swing: X equals the target’s maximum HP.

SPIDER: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Envenomed Snare — Passive
•	When the Spider’s Difficult Terrain causes a hostile character to spend additional movement: That character is Poisoned +1. ( No roll. )
Impassable Snare — Passive
•	Difficult Terrain created by the Spider’s Snare takes one additional Speed of movement per tile entered.
Pounce (◈) — Movement Reaction
•	1/Round — Condition: A hostile character performs a Movement Action while within a Snare, or enters a Snare during Movement.
•	Effect: The Spider performs a Movement Action. It must end this movement closer to the triggering character than it began.
Mobile Snare — Passive
•	At the beginning of the Spider’s turn: Choose any number of Snares in play. For each chosen Snare, move that Snare to a tile within Range 2 of that Snare. ( It doesn’t cross the intervening distance. )
Camouflage — Passive
•	While within a Snare: The Spider has Concealment 3.
Venom Spit (◆◆) — Ranged Attack
•	Range: 4 — DMG: [LV]+1
•	Has +1 Advantage against characters in Difficult Terrain.
•	On Hit: Inflicts Poisoned +2.

MIND FLAYER: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Extract Mind (◆) — Melee Spell Attack
•	1/Round — Range: 1 — DMG: 0
•	On Hit: If the target has no Focus remaining, deals bonus Chaos Damage equal to that character’s maximum HP.
Thought Skein (◆) — Spell
•	Range: 6
•	Target hostile character with less than half their maximum Focus remaining becomes Slowed +1. ( No roll. )
Wandering (◆) — Ranged Spell Attack
•	1/Round — Range: 6 — DMG: 0
•	On Hit: Target performs Move in a direction of the Mindflayer’s choice unless they spend [LV]+1 Focus.
Mindleech Trap (◆) — Trap
•	Charged — Range: 6
•	A Mindleech Trap deploys in a free space and is triggered by the hostile character that moves into that space.
•	Effect: The hostile character makes a Resistance Roll or loses [LV]+2 Focus. Whether or not that character Resists, they become Immobilized until the end of their next turn if they have less than half their maximum Focus remaining.
Nether Spike — Passive
•	A hostile character that spends Focus while adjacent to the Mindflayer takes that much Chaos Damage.
Detect Thoughts — Passive
•	The Mindflayer has Damage Resistance from characters with less than half their maximum Focus remaining.
Curse of the Silent — Passive
•	Compulsion targets hostile characters in a Size 1 Area within range.

Scorching Ray (◆) — Ranged Spell Attack

INVOKER: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Exploding Trap (◆) — Trap
•	Charged — Range: 5
•	An Exploding Trap deploys in a free space and is triggered by the hostile character that moves into that space.
•	Effect: The hostile character makes a Resistance Roll or suffers [LV]+4 Damage and Push 2 in a direction of the Invoker’s choice. A character that Resists suffers half damage and isn’t Knocked Back.
Blink (◆) — Movement Spell
•	Charged — Range: 6
•	Teleport to a free tile within range. Blink doesn’t provoke Reactions.
Shrapnel Blasts — Passive
•	Flame Strike has On Miss: The target character suffers Bleeding +1. ( No roll. )
Servant of the Secret Fire — Passive
•	Flame Strike becomes Charged and can be performed only with Initiative.
•	It gains On Damage: The target character makes a Resistance Roll or becomes Stunned +2.
Radiant Fire — Passive
•	The Invoker ignores the effects of Concealment and Stealth Actions.
•	Flame Strike leaves a lingering Size 1 Area until the Invoker next performs Flame Strike. A character in this area loses Concealment and can’t Hunker Down or perform Stealth Actions.

ABERRATION: OPTIONAL ABILITIES
Destroy Summon (◆◆) — Melee Weapon Attack
•	1/Round — Range: 1 — DMG: 0
•	Perform Destroy Summon only with Initiative.
•	On Hit: The target Summoned character is reduced to 0 HP. ( No roll. )
Destroy Hope (◆◆) — Spell
•	Charged — Range: 1
•	An adjacent hostile character makes a Resistance Roll. On a failure, for the rest of the encounter: that character can’t pay the Inspiration costs of non-Ultimate abilities.
Light Eater (◈) — Reaction
•	1/Round — Condition: A hostile character within Range 4 is reduced to 0 HP.
•	Effect: For the rest of the Quest – that character can’t use a Narrative Tag of the Aberration’s choice.
Hide (◈) — Stealth Reaction
•	An Aberration can Hide if it performed Hunker Down last turn.
•	1/Round — Condition: A hostile character begins their turn.
•	Effect: The character makes a Resistance Roll. On a failure, this turn, that character can’t target the Aberration with Actions that target only one character.
Infliction — Passive
•	The Aberration’s Backstab also inflicts Slowed +1 on a failed Resistance Roll.
Fleshmending — Passive
•	The Aberration recovers half its maximum HP when performing Refocus.
Skinshifting — Passive
•	The Aberration ignores Damage and Conditions from Actions that share a name with the last Action to inflict Damage or a Condition on the Aberration.
Aberrant Movement — Passive
•	The Aberration ignores Difficult Terrain and can move through hostile characters.
Mobius Trip — Passive
•	When the Aberration becomes adjacent to a map edge: The Aberration may Teleport to the opposite map edge, appearing in the nearest free tile. The Aberration ends the current Movement and loses all unspent AP.
